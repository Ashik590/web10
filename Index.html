ABOUT UNIVERSITY

Mr. Mirza Azam, MP a renowned personality of Jamalpur District has been elected Member of Parliament five times from Melandah-Madarganj constituency. Presently, he is the State minister of textile and jute of Bangladesh Government. Mr. Azam is renowned for his dedication for development of various socioeconomic activities especially in the sector of education

He is the founder of many educational institutions in his constituency and thereby paved the path for enlightening people from illiteracy and ignorance. He has been committed to promote the technical education for widening the national economy of Bangladesh and this willingness has been manifested through his decision to establish a science and technology based higher educational institute at Jamalpur.
